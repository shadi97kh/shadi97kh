Shadi Khodagholi

I am a Ph.D. student pursuing Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. My current research interests lie in federated learning and computer vision, particularly focusing on applications in healthcare systems. I am passionate about using these technologies to improve medical imaging and diagnostics, and I aim to contribute to advancements in healthcare through my work in these fields. My background includes a Master’s in Computer Science and a Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering, with a solid foundation in machine learning and object detection.
